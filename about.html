About Us

Discover our mission, vision, and commitment to excellence

Who We Are

Jadel Info tech is a leading computer training center dedicated to empowering students and professionals with cutting-edge technology skills. With over a decade of experience, we've successfully trained thousands of students to excel in their tech careers.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible, and industry-relevant computer training that bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world professional requirements. We aim to create a learning environment where students can develop both technical knowledge and practical expertise.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted technology training institution globally, known for producing skilled professionals who drive innovation and technological advancement. We envision a future where every student has the opportunity to transform their career through quality education and mentorship.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We maintain the highest standards in teaching and training
  • Innovation: We stay updated with the latest technology trends
  • Student-Centric: Our focus is always on student success and satisfaction
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency
  • Collaboration: We work together to achieve common goals
